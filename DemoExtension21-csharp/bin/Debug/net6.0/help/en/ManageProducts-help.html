Products

PalletBuilder supports three distinct product types for palletization. 

Box

Pallet

Separator

Separators are optional.  They can be added between box layers to improve stability. 

The list of defined products is displayed at the top areas of the screen.  A filter at the top right corner can be used to display only products of the selected type.  Selecting on a product in the list or pressing the New Product button will display the product's information.  The dimensions and the details of the product can be edited in this display.  A selected product from the list can be deleted using the Delete Product button.  A filter at the top right corner can display products of the selected type.

A gripper to handle the product can be selected in the “Gripper Used” dropdown menu.  If you do not have a gripper for your product, the gear icon can be pressed to go to the Gripper Manager screen.
