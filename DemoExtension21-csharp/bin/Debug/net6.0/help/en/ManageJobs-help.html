Manage Jobs

View a list of pick or place layer jobs.  Select a job from the list and press the Edit button to modify job or create a new job using the New button.  Once a job is created, it will appear in the list.  The status of a job is reflected in the Job Status column.  If a job's station has been deleted, the station will appear as "(Undefined)"

Job Statuses:

  • OK: The job is acceptable and ready to use.
  • Missing: The job is no longer on the controller.
  • Unrelated: The job exists on the controller but it was not generated by PalletBuilder or the job tag differs from "PALLETBUILDER". 
  • Modified: The job was modified outside of PalletBuilder.
  • Old: The job on the controller is older than the one noted in the database.  An older version might have been loaded into the controller. 

Generally, you may want to generate jobs that do not have an OK status.  This can be done using the Generate Job button.  Jobs can be deleted or copied using the Delete button or Duplicate buttons respectively.
